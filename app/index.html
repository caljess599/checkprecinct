FORM INSTRUCTIONS
General rules
  • You must enter a number; you cannot just enter a street name
  • The street type is never included (e.g., ST, DR, LN, CT) -- use just the name
  • Never include unit/apartment numbers; all residences that match a street address will be returned
Rules for "special" streets
  • Streets are entered without the compass quadrants, regardless of whether it appears before or after the name of the street (e.g., N, S, E, W, NW, etc.)
  • Whole-numbered streets are ended as ordinal numbers rather than words (e.g., 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, etc.)
  • Fractional-numbered streets are entered as fractions (e.g., 6 1/2)
  • Hypenated street names are entered with a space instead of the hyphen (e.g., Ridge McIntire)
